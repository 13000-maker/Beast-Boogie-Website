Houston's Baddest
B-Boy

Vicious Germz '01 Havikoro '13 AntiGravity '09–'15

28 years in the game. 80+ battles won. Millions taught. The movement never stops.

Anthony Silva picked up breaking in Houston and never looked back. He joined the legendary Vicious Germz crew in 2001 and later became a member of elite squad Havikoro in 2013 — two of the most respected breaking crews in the world.

From 2009 to 2015, he performed as an acro-dancer with AntiGravity, the official dunk team for the Houston Rockets — including a stint as mascot Clutch the Bear. He's graced stages at Art Basel, NBA All-Star Games, Red Bull BC One, and the world's biggest breakdance event, IBE in Europe.

“Dance taught me discipline, confidence, and how to express myself. I want every single person to feel that too — regardless of age or ability.”
